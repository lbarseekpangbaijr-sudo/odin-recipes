Dried Rice Recipe

Description

This dish is considered a working day food, as it is faster to get prepared and can be done simultaneously while doing other work.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Peppers
  • Fish
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and Boiled the rice
  2. Cut up peppers
  3. Fried or Salt Dry the fish
  4. Put the peppers in the oil and fry
  5. Keep some oil as a side season or appetizer of the dish
