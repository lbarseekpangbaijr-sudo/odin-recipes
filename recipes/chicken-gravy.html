Chicken Gravy

Description

This is a common delicacy consisting of sauce and chicken and is easier to cook. Anyone can learn and make this dish.

Ingredients

  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
  • Chicken
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut up and Boiled Chicken
  2. Cut up Peppers and Tomatoes
  3. Make Oil hot
  4. Put chicken in the hot oil
  5. Add cut up Peppers and Tomatoes
  6. Add water
  7. Finally add Seasonings
