Fried Beans Recipe

Description

This is a dish that is most beloved by locals, as it is belived to fill the stomach than most other food. and can be eaten with any suplementary meals along side it.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Beefs/Chicken
  • Peppers
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil the beans
  2. Cut up and boil the chicken or beef
  3. Cut up the Peppers
  4. Add oil to the boiled beans
  5. Then add the beefs and chicken to the beans
  6. When it's all done and cooked good in the stir. Add the seasonings.
